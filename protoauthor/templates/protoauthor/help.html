How to use our tool?

Our tool is an online prototype tool for designers to make a simple prototype. You can see various itmes on the navigation bar on the top. Drag them down on the canvas to make your own design! Right-click these items, you will see some of their functions and more specific help.

Here are some of the basic instructions:

1.Drag and drop: Please drag the element on the canvas.If you want to delete the items, make sure you have selected it (with red edges), then press 'delete' on the keyboard !

2.Copy and Paste: Make sure you have select the element (with red edges) and press ctrl+c and ctrl+v.

3.Mutiple-Select: Press 'Alt' on the keyboard first (Don't hold), then drag and cover the elements you want to select.

4.Resize: Drag the right-bottom corner of the item (Sorry we just have one angle to resize). Press 'Shift' to fix aspect ratio.

5.Menu:Click the icon on the top left to see your task again. Once you finish,click 'submit'.
